About DarkWaters

DarkWaters helps swimmers find nearby pools and track their workouts. Whether you're training hard or just having fun, we've got you covered.

We aim to make swimming easier and more motivating by giving you a simple space to log swims and stay on track.

🏊 What We Offer

  • Find public and private pools in your area
  • Log and track your swim workouts easily

📍 How It Works

Sign up, search for a pool, add your workouts — that’s it. No clutter, just swimming.

We’re building a space made for swimmers, and you're part of it.
